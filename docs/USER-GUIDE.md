# User Guide — TIKTON Portal

## 1. Register & sign in

1. Visit `/register`, enter email + password (≥8 chars).
2. Verify your email (mock environment: link auto-shown after submit).
3. Click **Sign in** on `/login`.

## 2. Choose a plan

Open `/pricing`, pick Free / Pro / Business. Pro and Business require payment (production); in dev, the upgrade is instant.

## 3. Submit a video

1. Go to `/submit`.
2. Paste a TikTok URL (e.g. `https://www.tiktok.com/@user/video/123`).
3. Select target language and subtitle style.
4. Click **Submit** — you'll be redirected to the task detail.

## 4. Track progress

- `/tasks` lists all your tasks with filters by status.
- Click a row to open the detail page.
- The pipeline shows 10 steps; progress auto-refreshes every 5s.

## 5. Download

When status is **completed**, the **Download** link appears in the detail page.
